# Mission

## What this app is

**Casita Construcción** is a mobile-first project management tool for small construction projects managed by a tight-knit team (owner, family members, foreman).

## Core problem it solves

Keep the project's economy stable by giving the team a single place to:
- Register every material purchase, payroll payment, and income entry
- Know at any moment: *"¿Cuánto dinero me queda y el proyecto está sano?"*

## Who uses it

- **Owner** — full control over budget, decisions, and reports
- **Team members** (family, foreman) — log expenses, materials, and worker payments without needing separate tools

## What it is NOT

- Not a SaaS product
- Not multi-role or permission-gated — everyone with access sees everything
- Not a full ERP — scoped to 2–5 construction projects maximum

## Success criteria

1. Any team member can log an expense in under 30 seconds
2. The owner can share a financial summary without needing a spreadsheet
3. The project never goes over budget silently — the app warns before it happens
